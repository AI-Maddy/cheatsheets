====================
NB-IoT / LTE-M / 5G NR
====================

.. note::
   This is a cheatsheet for NB-IoT, LTE-M, and 5G NR protocols.

**NB-IoT**
- Narrowband IoT, cellular LPWAN
- Low power, deep coverage
- Used for smart meters, sensors

**LTE-M**
- LTE for Machines, cellular IoT
- Higher data rate than NB-IoT
- Mobility, voice support

**5G NR**
- 5G New Radio, next-gen cellular
- Ultra-low latency, high throughput
- Massive IoT, URLLC, eMBB

**Key Features**
- Licensed spectrum, secure
- Global coverage (cellular)
- Power saving modes

**Mnemonic**: "Narrowband, Machines, Next Radio"

**Common Use Cases**
- Smart cities, asset tracking, industrial IoT

**Pros**
- Reliable, scalable, future-proof

**Cons**
- SIM/subscription required
- Network-dependent
