LIN
===

**Domain:** Automotive

**Description:**
Low-cost, single-wire for non-critical sensors/actuators (e.g., windows, mirrors).

**Key Features:**
- 🔗 Single-wire, low-cost
- 🧩 Master-slave protocol
- 🛠️ Up to 16 nodes per bus
- 🕒 Low speed (up to 20 kbps)

**Physical/Electrical Layer:**
- 🔌 Single-wire, open collector
- ⚡ Data rates: up to 20 kbps

**Topology:**
- 🌐 Bus topology

**Use Cases:**
- 🚗 Windows, mirrors, seat controls
- 🛠️ Non-critical sensors/actuators

**Pros:**
- 💸 Very low cost
- 🧩 Simple wiring, easy to implement

**Cons:**
- 🐢 Low speed, not for critical systems
- ⚠️ Limited scalability

**Standard:**
- 📜 LIN Consortium Specification

**Typical Applications:**
- 🚗 Entry-level and mid-range vehicles

**Memorization Tip:**
<span style="color:#1976d2;">Think of LIN as the "cheap chat line" for your car's windows and seats—simple, slow, but gets the job done!</span>
