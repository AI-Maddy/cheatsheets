V2X (DSRC/C-V2X)
================

**Domain:** Automotive

**Description:**
Vehicle-to-vehicle/infrastructure communication in connected/autonomous driving.

**Key Features:**
- 📡 Wireless vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V), vehicle-to-infrastructure (V2I)
- 🚦 Improves safety, traffic efficiency
- 🔄 DSRC (802.11p) and C-V2X (cellular)
- 🛡️ Low latency, high reliability

**Physical/Electrical Layer:**
- 📶 DSRC: 5.9 GHz, C-V2X: LTE/5G
- ⚡ Data rates: up to several Mbps

**Topology:**
- 🌐 Ad hoc, mesh, infrastructure

**Use Cases:**
- 🚗 Collision avoidance, traffic alerts
- 🛠️ Autonomous driving, smart cities

**Pros:**
- 🚀 Real-time, direct communication
- 🧩 Enhances safety, efficiency

**Cons:**
- 🏗️ Infrastructure rollout required
- ⚠️ Interoperability, security challenges

**Standard:**
- 📜 IEEE 802.11p (DSRC), 3GPP (C-V2X)

**Typical Applications:**
- 🚗 Connected/autonomous vehicles, smart infrastructure

**Memorization Tip:**
<span style="color:#1976d2;">Think of V2X as "cars talking to everything"—roads, lights, and each other for a safer drive!</span>
