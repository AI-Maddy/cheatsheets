MOST
====

**Domain:** Automotive

**Description:**
Fiber-optic for multimedia/infotainment (declining but legacy in premium vehicles).

	<div style="background-color:#e3f2fd; border-radius:10px; padding:16px; margin-bottom:16px;">

**Key Features:**
- 🎶 Fiber-optic, high-speed multimedia bus
- 📺 Supports audio, video, data
- 🔄 Ring or daisy-chain topology
- 🛡️ Low EMI, high reliability

**Physical/Electrical Layer:**
- 🔦 Plastic optical fiber (POF)
- ⚡ Data rates: up to 150 Mbps

**Topology:**
- 🔄 Ring, daisy-chain

**Use Cases:**
- 🚗 Infotainment, premium audio, video streaming
- 🛠️ Legacy multimedia in high-end vehicles

**Pros:**
- 🎵 High quality, low noise
- 🛡️ Immune to EMI

**Cons:**
- 🏗️ Expensive, complex
- ⚠️ Being replaced by Automotive Ethernet

**Standard:**
- 📜 MOST Cooperation Specification

**Typical Applications:**
- 🚗 Premium vehicles, legacy infotainment

**Memorization Tip:**
<span style="color:#1976d2;">Think of MOST as "music on a light beam"—your car's concert hall, delivered by fiber!</span>
