I²C
===

**Domain:** Embedded/Industrial

**Description:**
2-wire, multi-master serial bus.

.. raw:: html

	<div style="background-color:#e3f2fd; border-radius:10px; padding:16px; margin-bottom:16px;">
	<h2 style="color:#1565c0;">🔌🧩 I²C (Inter-Integrated Circuit)</h2>
	<b>Domain:</b> <span style="color:#1976d2;">Embedded/Industrial</span><br>
	<b>Mnemonic:</b> <span style="color:#388e3c;">"I Squared Connects"</span><br>
	<b>Icon:</b> 🔌🧩<br>
	</div>

**Key Features:**
- 🧩 2-wire (SDA, SCL), multi-master
- 🔄 Supports multiple slaves (up to 127)
- 🕒 Clock stretching for flow control
- 🛡️ Simple, low-cost, low-speed

**Physical/Electrical Layer:**
- 🔌 Open-drain, pull-up resistors
- ⚡ Data rates: 100 kbps (standard), 400 kbps (fast), 3.4 Mbps (high-speed)

**Topology:**
- 🌐 Bus topology

**Use Cases:**
- 📟 Sensors, EEPROMs, RTCs, small peripherals
- 🛠️ Board-level communication

**Pros:**
- 🧩 Simple, easy to implement
- 💸 Low cost, minimal wiring

**Cons:**
- 🐢 Low speed, short distance
- ⚠️ Susceptible to noise

**Standard:**
- 📜 NXP I²C Specification

**Typical Applications:**
- 🖥️ Embedded systems, microcontrollers, sensors

**Memorization Tip:**
<span style="color:#1976d2;">Think of I²C as the "two-wire handshake"—simple, friendly, and everywhere on a circuit board!</span>
