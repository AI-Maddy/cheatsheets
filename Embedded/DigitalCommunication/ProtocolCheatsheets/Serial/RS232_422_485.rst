RS-232 / RS-422 / RS-485
========================

**Domain:** Embedded/Industrial

**Description:**
Classic serial standards (differential/long distance).

**Key Features:**
- 🔌 Serial, point-to-point (RS-232), multi-drop (RS-485)
- 📡 Differential signaling (RS-422/485)
- 🛡️ Long distance, robust
- 🧩 Simple, low-cost

**Physical/Electrical Layer:**
- ⚡ RS-232: ±12V, single-ended
- ⚡ RS-422/485: differential, up to 1.2 km
- ⚡ Data rates: up to 1 Mbps (RS-485)

**Topology:**
- 🌐 Point-to-point (RS-232/422), bus (RS-485)

**Use Cases:**
- 🖥️ Industrial automation, PLCs
- 🛠️ Legacy PC serial ports, modems

**Pros:**
- 🧩 Simple, cheap, reliable
- 🛡️ Long range (RS-485)

**Cons:**
- 🐢 Low speed (RS-232)
- ⚠️ Susceptible to noise (RS-232)

**Standard:**
- 📜 EIA/TIA-232/422/485

**Typical Applications:**
- 🏭 Factories, industrial equipment, legacy PCs

**Memorization Tip:**
<span style="color:#1976d2;">Think of RS-232/422/485 as the "old-school serial lines"—simple, tough, and everywhere in industry!</span>
