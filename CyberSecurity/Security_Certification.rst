TL;DR - Quick Reference
=======================

**Certification Types:**

- **Common Criteria (CC):** International security evaluation (EAL1-EAL7)
- **FIPS 140-3:** Cryptographic module validation
- **UL 2900:** Cybersecurity for network-connectable products

Common Criteria EAL Levels
============================

.. code-block:: text

    EAL1: Functionally tested
    EAL2: Structurally tested (most commercial products)
    EAL3: Methodically tested and checked
    EAL4: Methodically designed, tested, and reviewed (smart cards)
    EAL5: Semiformally designed and tested
    EAL6: Semiformally verified design and tested
    EAL7: Formally verified design and tested (military)

**FIPS 140-3 Levels:**

.. code-block:: text

    Level 1: Basic security (software crypto)
    Level 2: Physical tamper-evidence (seals, coatings)
    Level 3: Tamper-resistant (zeroization on intrusion)
    Level 4: Complete envelope protection (active tamper response)

Certification Process
======================

**Timeline:** 12-18 months for Common Criteria EAL4

**Cost:** $100,000-$500,000 depending on EAL level
